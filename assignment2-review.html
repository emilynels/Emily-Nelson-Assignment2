My Favorite Shots from the Trilogy

My Review of the Trilogy

Blue: 8.5/10

It is defintely the most "aesthetic" of of the three. This one had a lot of symbolism like the blue mobile and the cross necklace. The acting in this one was phenominal, especially from Juliette Binoche, who portrayed grief and loneliness so well. It was really easy to get engaged with the story, and Binoche's acting kept my attention the whole time. I really enjoyed her scenes with Oliver while they were working on finishing the composition.

White: 7.5/10

This movie was a little bit of a mess, but in the funniest way. This film is classified as an "anti-comedy", which I think it does really well. There are a lot of elements here that are just absurd and unrelated to the plot. This made it kind of confusing but also funny at the same time. Karol Karol was so chaotic and I loved it, and I'm a big Julie Delpy fan so I enjoyed watching her. Overall I liked this one, I just didn't feel as connected to the characters or the story as the other two.

Red: 10/10

This is one of my favorite movies ever. I've only seen it once because I'm scared if I watch it again I won't connect with it the same way as I did the first time. All three of these films are dialogue heavy, but this one is the most. I am fascinated by the chance encounter and connection with someone you would never expect. This film really highlights the randomness and beauty of connection, as well as missed connections. So beautiful and real from beginning to end.
