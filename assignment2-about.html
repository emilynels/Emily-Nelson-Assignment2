About the Trilogy

Three Colors: Blue

Three Colors: Blue is about Liberty. Set in Paris, the film follows Julie, a woman whose husband and child are killed in a car accident. Filled with grief and sadness, Julie decides to try and live her life in isolation. But encounters with people from her past make her realize that she can't escape connection.

Actor Character Role
Juliette Binoche Julie Main Character
Benoît Régent Oliver Julie's long-time friend
Florence Pernel Sandrine Julie's mother

Three Colors: White

Three Colors: White is about equality. Karol Karol, a barber living in Paris, just went through a divorce with his wife, Dominique, and is left with nothing. He decides to return to his native country Poland, where he plots to take revenge on his ex-wife and restore the equality in his life.

Actor Character Role
Zbigniew Zamachowski Karol Karol Main Character
Julie Delpy Dominique Karol's wife
Janusz Gajos Mikolaj Karol's best friend

Three Colors: Red

Three Colors: Red is about fraternity. Red follows a college student named Valentine, who lives in Geneva. One night, she meets a man named Joseph during a chance encounter. While at first they seem to have nothing in common, they begin to develop a strong connection.
